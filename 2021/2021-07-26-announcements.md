## NIH Data Sharing and Reuse Seminar Series
The NIH Office of Data Science Strategy hosts a series of seminars on data sharing and reuse on the second Friday of every month (CFDE Member Deanne Taylor presented in May). There are schedules, registration, recordings, and contact details available at https://datascience.nih.gov/data-sharing-and-reuse-seminar-series. Presentations are open to everyone.
